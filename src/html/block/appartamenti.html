Accomodation

Flat info

The Residence has 8 apartments, 4 studio flats and 4 two-roomed flats, furnished in a good taste with bathroom, kitchenette, fridge, microwave oven, TV color, and kitchenware. Each apartment has a terrace or a balcony with a wonderful view.
